# Getting Started — A Simple Guide

Welcome! This guide walks you through using the Inventory Management app for the
first time. **No technical knowledge needed** — just follow the steps in order.

---

## 1. Create your account

1. Open the app in your web browser (your team will give you the link, e.g.
   `https://your-app.vercel.app`).
2. Click **Register / Sign up**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Company name** — your business name (this creates your private workspace).
   - **Your name**
   - **Email** and a **password** (at least 8 characters).
4. Click **Create account**. You are now the **Admin** of your company.

> The first person to register for a company becomes the **Admin**. Admins can do
> everything, including inviting other team members.

---

## 2. Log in

Next time you visit, click **Sign in**, enter your email and password, and you'll
land on your **Dashboard**.

---

## 3. Set up your business (recommended order)

Think of this like stocking a new shop. Do these once at the start:

| Step | What to do | Where |
|------|------------|-------|
| 1 | **Add categories** — groups for your items (e.g. "Beverages", "Snacks"). | Categories page |
| 2 | **Add products** — each item you sell, with its price and starting stock. | Products page |
| 3 | **Add suppliers** — the businesses you buy stock from. | Suppliers page |
| 4 | **Add customers** — the people or businesses you sell to (optional). | Customers page |

You can always add more later.

---

## 4. Everyday tasks

### Recording a purchase (stock coming IN)
When new stock arrives from a supplier:
1. Go to **Purchases → New Purchase**.
2. Pick the supplier and add the products and quantities you received.
3. Save. Your stock counts go **up** automatically.

### Recording a sale (stock going OUT)
When you sell something:
1. Go to **Sales → New Sale**.
2. Pick the customer (or leave blank for a walk-in) and add the items sold.
3. Save. Your stock counts go **down** automatically, and an **invoice is created**.

### Printing an invoice
1. Go to **Invoices**.
2. Find the sale and click **Download PDF**. You can print or email it to your customer.

---

## 5. Keeping an eye on things

- **Dashboard** — a quick snapshot: total sales, number of products, and low-stock alerts.
- **Inventory** — see current stock levels and a history of every stock change.
- **Notifications** — you'll be alerted when a product runs **low** or **out of stock**.
- **Reports** — see how your business is doing (sales, purchases, profit) and
  download the numbers as Excel or PDF.

---

## 6. Adding your team (Admins only)

1. Go to **Settings → Users** (or the Users page).
2. Invite a teammate with their email — they'll get an **Employee** account.
3. Employees can record sales and purchases but can't change company-wide settings.

---

## 7. Managing your plan

Under **Settings**, you can view your subscription plan:

- **Free** — small teams (up to 2 users, 50 products).
- **Basic** — growing businesses (up to 10 users, 1,000 products, reports & exports).
- **Premium** — unlimited users and products, priority support.

---

## Quick tips

- 🔒 Your data is private to your company — no one else can see it.
- 💾 Everything saves automatically when you click Save.
- ❓ Stuck? Start from the **Dashboard** — every section is one click away in the
  left sidebar.

That's it — you're ready to go! 🎉
